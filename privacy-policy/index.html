DreamWave Journal App

Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 9, 2026

DreamWave Journal is a personal journaling app designed to help you document and reflect on experiences such as dreams, lucid dreams, and other inner experiences. The app is designed with privacy in mind. Your journal entries remain stored on your device, and we collect limited information necessary to support app functionality and security. By using DreamWave Journal, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

1. Our Approach to Privacy

Your journal entries and personal reflections belong to you. DreamWave Journal is designed to give you control over your content, including built-in features that allow you to export, print, or share entries using your device's standard sharing options. By default, entries are stored locally within the DreamWave Journal app on your device. This means your entries are accessible only through the app itself and are not visible as separate files on your device unless you choose to export them.

2. Information We Collect

Account Information (Firebase Authentication)

To provide secure access to your account:

  • Email address
  • Authentication credentials managed by Firebase Authentication
  • Optional display name

This information is managed by Firebase Authentication, a Google service with industry-standard security.

Account Setup and Login Information

We require account creation to use DreamWave Journal for the following reasons:

  • Secure access: Your account ensures your locally stored data is protected and only accessible to you, even if multiple users share the same device
  • Subscription management: Your account links your subscription and purchase entitlements to ensure proper access to premium features
  • Account recovery: Enables password reset and account recovery options
  • Data separation: Prevents mixing of data between different users on shared devices

Important: Your account credentials are used solely for authentication and subscription management. We do not use your account to sync, upload, or access your journal content stored on your device.

Internal User Identifier

DreamWave uses an internal, app-specific user identifier to manage local data and personalize your experience within the app. This identifier is stored locally on your device and is not shared with third parties. Authentication providers (such as Firebase) may supply their own identifiers for login purposes, which are handled separately.

Local Data (Stored Only On Your Device)

The following stays on your device and is never uploaded to our servers:

  • Journal entries (all content you write)
  • Your personal Symbol Dictionary
  • Plans and routine tracking
  • AI conversation history
  • Custom breathing patterns

We cannot access this data. It remains on your device unless you choose to export or share it using available features.

User-Generated Media and Local Storage

Photos, audio recordings, images, and other media that you attach to journal entries or insights are stored locally on your device. DreamWave does not automatically upload or transmit user-generated media to external servers. You may choose to export or share your own content (for example, via email, file sharing, or cloud services available on your device). Any content you choose to export or share is controlled entirely by you and is not retained by DreamWave.

Exported Content and External Storage

When you choose to export, share, or back up journal entries, images, audio recordings, or other content using your device's sharing or backup features (such as email, cloud storage, or file transfer tools), that content is handled according to the privacy policies of those external services. DreamWave does not receive, store, or manage exported content once it leaves your device.

AI Usage (Optional)

The AI feature is available with each journal entry. Only when you choose to use AI features in the Wisdom tab, the following is sent to Anthropic (maker of Claude):

  • The current journal text content and title within the "Journal" tab you're working with only
  • The AI conversation history for that specific entry only
  • Experience type selected on "Journal" tab

Not automatically included:

  • Other journal entries
  • Your Symbol Library
  • "Objective" and "Subjective" tab content
  • "Wisdom" tab content
  • Images or audio recordings

Third-Party AI Providers: When you choose to use AI-powered features within DreamWave, relevant content (such as the current journal entry text and AI conversation context) is transmitted via our AI routing partner, OpenRouter, to a selected third-party AI model provider (such as Anthropic's Claude). OpenRouter and AI model providers such as Anthropic operate independently and maintain their own privacy and data-handling policies, which may change over time. DreamWave does not control how third-party providers retain, process, or store data once it has been transmitted for AI processing. We encourage users to review the most current versions of these policies directly:

  • OpenRouter Privacy Policy: https://openrouter.ai/privacy
  • Anthropic (Claude) Data Retention Policy: https://privacy.claude.com/en/articles/10023548

DreamWave does not store AI prompt content or AI responses on its own servers by default. AI interactions are handled locally within the app unless you explicitly choose to export, share, or save content externally. All AI usage within DreamWave is optional and user-initiated.

AI Model Training and Retention: AI model providers may retain or process prompts and responses according to their own privacy policies and user-selected preferences. DreamWave does not control whether AI providers use submitted content for service improvement, retention, or training purposes. Users are encouraged to review and manage any available data retention or training settings directly with the AI provider.

AI Processing Details: When you use AI features, data is sent to Anthropic's Claude API with the following safeguards:

  • End-to-end encryption in transit (TLS/HTTPS)
  • Anthropic states that user content is not stored after processing and is not used for model training.
  • Processed in a manner designed to support compliance with applicable privacy laws such as GDPR and CCPA.
  • You can view Anthropic's Data Processing Addendum at: anthropic.com/legal

AI Conversation Context:

  • Only the current entry's AI conversation is maintained
  • AI conversations are stored locally on your device
  • You can clear AI conversation history anytime from within the app
  • Clearing history does not affect your journal entries

Technical Data

We collect minimal technical information:

  • Device type and operating system (for compatibility)
  • App version

We do NOT use analytics platforms to track your behavior, feature usage, or time spent in the app.

3. How We Use Your Information

Account Authentication: Secure login across devices, password recovery, account management.

App Functionality:

  • Store and display your local journal entries
  • Process AI requests when you choose to use them
  • Enable breathwork and binaural beat features
  • Store and display plans and routines
  • Send local notifications you've set up

App Improvement: Fix crashes and technical issues, ensure device compatibility, develop features that serve your practice.

What we DON'T do:

  • Analyze your journal content or dream patterns
  • Track which features you use
  • Build behavioral or advertising profiles
  • Share your data with marketing companies
  • Use your experiences for research without consent

4. Sharing Your Information

Firebase Authentication (Google): Email and encrypted password for account access. Firebase does not access your journal content.

Anthropic (Claude AI): Only when you use AI features. Only the specific journal entry and conversation context. Anthropic does not retain your content after processing.

Apple App Store / Google Play: Subscription and purchase information. Handled entirely by Apple/Google—we don't see payment details. DreamWave does not receive access to Apple or Google account identifiers beyond subscription entitlement confirmation.

We NEVER:

  • Sell or rent your personal information
  • Share data with advertising networks or data brokers
  • Provide your journal content to third parties
  • Use your data for purposes beyond those stated here

5. Data Storage & Security

5.1 Local Storage

Your journal entries and personal content are stored on your device using secure local database technology. This data remains private and under your control.

5.2 Device Backups

Your device's automatic backup system (iCloud, Google Backup) may include DreamWave data. We don't control these backups. To keep data fully private, you can exclude DreamWave from automatic backups in your device settings.

5.3 Account Security

We take reasonable measures to help protect your account. Account authentication is handled through Firebase Authentication, a secure authentication service provided by Google. We do not store your password directly. You are responsible for maintaining the confidentiality of your login credentials and for all activity that occurs under your account. We recommend using a strong, unique password and not sharing your credentials with anyone.

5.4 No Cloud Syncing or Backup by DreamWave

We want to be clear about what we DO NOT do:

  • We do NOT automatically sync your journal content to cloud servers
  • We do NOT back up your journal entries to our servers
  • We do NOT have access to your journal content on our servers
  • We do NOT store copies of your entries remotely

Your journal data remains exclusively on your device. If you lose your device or uninstall the app without exporting your data first, we cannot recover it for you. Recommendation: Regularly export important entries using the app's export features to maintain your own backups.

5.5 Network Security

  • All data sent to AI services is encrypted in transit (HTTPS/TLS)
  • No automatic cloud syncing of journal content

6. Your Rights & Choices

You have complete control over your data:

Access & Review: View all your journal entries and content; review your account information.

Edit & Delete: Modify or delete any journal entry; modify or delete any symbol from Symbol Library; clear AI conversation history; delete all local data or your entire account.

Export: Export entries to PDF; export AI conversations to PDF; share entries via your device's native sharing features.

Disable Features: Disable notifications; manage app permissions in device settings.

Delete Your Account: Contact us at support@dreamwavejournal.com. We'll delete your Firebase Authentication data, and you'll be instructed to clear local data from your device.

7. App Permissions

DreamWave may request these device permissions:

  • Microphone (Optional): For voice journaling features (if you choose to record audio)
  • Camera (Optional): For adding photos to journal entries
  • Photo Library (Optional): For selecting existing photos to add to entries
  • Notifications (Optional): For reminders and alerts you set within the app
  • Face ID (Optional): For quick convenient login and privacy between sessions
  • Storage/Files (Optional): For exporting journal entries as PDF or other formats
  • Background App Refresh (Optional): For notification delivery and scheduled reminders

All permissions are optional. You can grant or revoke them anytime in your device settings. The core journaling functionality works without these permissions, though specific features (like voice recording or photo attachments) will require their respective permissions.

We do NOT request or require: Location services, Contacts access, Calendar access, Health data access, Bluetooth or NFC.

8. Children's Privacy

DreamWave is intended for users aged 13 and older. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has created an account, please contact us immediately at support@dreamwavejournal.com and we will delete it.

9. Health & Wellness Disclaimer

DreamWave is a personal exploration tool, not a medical or therapeutic service. Our features—journaling, breathwork, binaural beats, and AI-guided reflection—are designed for personal insight and self-exploration. They are not:

  • A substitute for professional mental health care
  • A diagnostic tool for psychological or sleep disorders
  • Medical or therapeutic treatment
  • A replacement for licensed counseling

Our AI uses a Socratic approach—asking reflective questions to guide you toward your own insights. It cannot and does not provide therapy or clinical advice, psychological diagnosis, crisis intervention, or professional mental health support.

If you're experiencing distressing dreams or nightmares, mental health challenges, sleep disorders, or psychological distress, please consult a qualified healthcare provider or mental health professional.

Crisis Resources: National Suicide Prevention Lifeline: 988 · Crisis Text Line: Text HOME to 741741 · Emergency Services: 911 (US) or your local emergency number.

By using DreamWave's AI features for personal reflection on difficult emotions or experiences, you acknowledge this is for self-exploration only and not a substitute for professional care.

10. Data Retention

Active Accounts: Account information remains in Firebase Authentication while your account is active. Local journal data persists on your device until you delete it. AI conversation history stays on your device until cleared.

Deleted Accounts: Firebase Authentication data is deleted immediately. You're responsible for clearing local data from your device. Any exported files remain wherever you saved them.

Crash Logs: Retained for 12 months to identify recurring issues. Contains no personally identifiable information.

11. International Users

DreamWave is available globally. Your data may be processed in different regions. Firebase Authentication operates in Google's global infrastructure and complies with international data protection laws. Anthropic (Claude) processes AI requests in compliant data centers and maintains GDPR and privacy law standards. Your journal content stays on your device regardless of location, with no automatic international transfer.

Rights for European Users (GDPR): Right to access, correct, delete, restrict processing, data portability, object to processing, and lodge a complaint with your supervisory authority.

Rights for California Residents (CCPA/CPRA): Right to know what data we collect, right to delete your data, right to opt out of data sales (we don't sell data), and right to non-discrimination.

We extend these rights to all users, regardless of location.

11.5 Data Portability and User Control

You have complete ownership and control over your journal content. Export Options: Export individual entries as PDF; export AI conversations as PDF; share entries through your device's native sharing options. All exported data is in standard formats you can read without DreamWave. You can export your data anytime before deleting your account, and you control where exported files are stored. No proprietary formats lock you into our app.

Manual Backup Recommendation: Since your journal is stored locally and we don't provide cloud backup, we recommend regularly exporting important entries, saving exports to your preferred backup location, and maintaining copies before switching devices or uninstalling.
